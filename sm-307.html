Chain-Driven Pipe Cold Cutting & Beveling Machine

325–1420 mm

A chain-driven pipe cold cutting and beveling machine for pipes from 325 to 1420 mm in diameter. Spark-free, in-service cutting that leaves a weld-ready edge on oil and gas pipelines.

It cuts the pipe with an angle milling cutter and prepares the weld edge (beveling) simultaneously — no heat, no sparks and no heat-affected zone (HAZ), suitable for hazardous, hydrocarbon-containing areas where hot work is prohibited.

Supplied as a complete set: machine with electric motor, two angle milling cutters (Ø135 and Ø140) and documentation; a coolant-supply system and guide template are supplied on request, by arrangement with the customer.
